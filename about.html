ABOUT US

TEACHERS ACADEMY AFRICA
 is set up as an organization and
 an EdTech solutions provider with
 a strong commitment and focus in
 capacity-building and professional
 development of teachers.

We explore innovative approach with technology to create effective solutions and services which are tailored to address the skill gap of educators by upskilling them to prepare their students for the 21st Century and the future of work through innovative pedagogical approaches and development of relevant fluencies and skill sets.

TEACHERS ACADEMY AFRICA COMMUNITY OF EDUCATORS

Our Community of Educators is our Professional Learning Network (PLN) or Professional Learning Community (PLC) for teachers, educators, parents, school head and leaders, education stakeholders, education policy makers and every other person interested in the education space.

The Community is an avenue for all educators to come together to learn, share ideas, share opportunities, collaborate and connect with other professionals in the education space.


Our core areas of focus includes:

* Teachers/Educators Capacity Development.

* 21st century skillsets and fluencies for teaching and learning.

* The Future of Work.

* Innovative pedagogical approaches.

* Lifelong and Lifewide learning.

* Leveraging technology for Teaching and Learning.


As a community of practice, we are very much interested in:

* Fostering a culture of collaboration,

* Removing every barrier to success, and

* Focusing on desired results.
