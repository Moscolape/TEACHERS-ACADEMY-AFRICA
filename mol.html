MEET OUR CREATIVE LEAD

OMOTAYO OLORUNFEMI is a teacher, capacity development expert, speaker and thought leader with over ten years' experience in the Education sector with expertise in O'level and A'level Chemistry, capacity development and use of technology in education for teachers and students.

He possesses an O.N.D and B.TECH (Hons.) in Chemical Engineering, PGDE in Science Education and M.Ed (in view) in Education Planning and Administration. Olorunfemi is a Google Certified Educator (Level 1), a Certified Microsoft Innovative Educator, a registered member of the Teachers Registration Council of Nigeria (TCRN) and also a member of the Microsoft Educators community and a WAEC Examiner in Chemistry.

Olorunfemi has partnered with LEARNFACTORY NIGERIA in implementing the Google Impact Challenge and Google Online Safety Roadshow projects in 2019. He currently partners with Striving for Greatness EdTech Initiative in implementing TEACHERS CONFERENCE NG project majorly sponsored by Google.

He is the host of Rethinking Education Podcast (featured on Google, Apple, Spotify, Breaker, PocketCasts and RadioPublic podcast platforms), the creator of TEACHERS ACADEMY AFRICA Youtube channel, the convener of Rethinking Education Summit and the Organizer and Co-Organizer of TEDxAbayi and TEDxAbiaStatePoly respectively.

Olorunfemi holds a certificate of Honor from the National Mathematical Centre, a certificate of completion of Artificial Intelligence and python programming masterclass from Data Science Nigeria, an award certificate for the implementation of the Millennium Development Goals (MDGs) advocacy program from NYSC, a capacity building certificate on entrepreneurship from the Bank of Industry (BOI) and was one of the top eight(8) finalists nationwide at the FATE FOUNDATION aspiring entrepreneurs (Digital), a program sponsored by FACEBOOK Nigeria in 2019.

Olorunfemi is very passionate about Youth development, digitization and democratization of quality learning content in solving the global learning crisis. He is a firm believer in the capacity of Educators all over Africa to create the future they seek to see in the Education industry and an advocate of SDGs Goal 4,5 and 8.

Olorunfemi loves reading, travelling, speaking, mentoring and engaging in Community building Initiatives. He is the Founder of TEACHERS ACADEMY AFRICA.

"To equip and empower teachers/educators with relevant knowledge. Great teacher, Great Nation."

~ Director, TeachersAcademy Africa
